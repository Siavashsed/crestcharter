Three separate jet charters booked differently versus one comprehensive arrangement can have wildly different implications.

**Asset protection mechanics**: High-spend individuals often expose themselves unnecessarily. The right trust structures, booking entities, and liability positioning prevent problems that become expensive only when they materialize—which is precisely when they're hardest to fix.
